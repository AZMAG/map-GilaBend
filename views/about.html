About the Gila Bend Zoning Viewer

The Gila Bend Zoning Viewer is an interactive mapping application that displays updated zoning information for the Town of Gila Bend.

Navigate around the map using your mouse

  • To Pan (move the map), simply click and hold your mouse, then drag the map.
  • To Center and Zoom In on a location, double click the location.
  • To Zoom In using the mouse scroll wheel, scroll forward.
  • To Zoom Out using the mouse scroll wheel, scroll backward.
  • Select an area to Zoom to by holding down the SHIFT key while drawing a box with the mouse.
  • Select an area to Zoom out from by holding down the SHIFT + CTRL keys while drawing a box with the mouse.
  • Use the arrow keys to pan
  • Use + key to zoom in a level
  • Use - key to zoom out a level
  • Click on a map feature to reveal further information.

Data Source

  • Town of Gila Bend Zoning
  • Parcel data provided by the Maricopa Assessors Office
  • Floodways data from FEMA and The Flood Control District of Maricopa County
  • Maricopa Association of Governments Employer data 2013
  • Aerial Imagery provided by ESRI
  • Street map provided by ESRI
