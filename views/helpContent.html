The Content describes the contents of the current map. The contents of the map are organized into layers that group similar types of geographic features.

The layers for the map are located in the Layer Options drop-down panel.

The map contains the following layers:
  • Street map information
  • Aerial Imagery
  • Maricopa County Parcels
  • Town of Gila Bend Boundary
  • Town of Gila Bend Pending Flood Zones
  • Town of Gila Bend Flood Zone
  • Town of Gila Bend Zoning
  • Town of Gila Bend Addresses
You can change the display of the maps content in the following ways:
  • Change the visibility of a layer by checking or un-checking the layers check box.
  • Change the transparency by moving the layers slider. Moving the slider to the left will increase the layers transparency which will allow for seeing the contents underneath the layer. *Available only for the Flood Zone and Zoning layers
